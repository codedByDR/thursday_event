06

Multi-Agent AI System

Powered by LangGraph stateful multi-agent system

STHENIA utilizes a sophisticated LangGraph-based multi-agent architecture with conditional branching and human-in-the-loop checkpoints for maximum reliability and accuracy.

  • Request Agent: Intelligent request handling and justification generation
  • Counselling Agent: Mental health support, burnout detection, and wellbeing check-ins
  • POSH Agent: Anonymous harassment reporting and policy violation detection
  • Shift Safety Agent: Night shift buddy system and transport coordination
  • Maternity Agent: Return-to-work planning and phase-based transition guidance
  • Meal Planner Agent: Nutritional planning and grocery management
  • Policy Checker: Document verification and compliance monitoring
07

Executive Summary & Goals

Core objectives and system capabilities

STHENIA enables employees to request arrangements such as work from home, shift changes, and caregiving support with AI-guided assistance.

  • Enable employees to submit flexible work requests quickly and clearly
  • Improve request quality via AI guidance and professionally formatted justifications
  • Ensure routing, approvals, and escalations follow defined policy and hierarchy rules
  • Increase consistency and fairness across teams through standardized criteria

Agent System Support Capabilities:

  • Mental Health & Wellbeing: Counselling sessions, burnout detection, wellbeing check-ins, and crisis intervention
  • Personal Life Management: Meal planning, grocery reminders, daily todos, and alarm management
  • Workplace Safety: Shift safety monitoring, night shift buddy system, and transport coordination
  • Career Development: Learning modules, micro-learning sessions, and professional growth tracking
  • Policy & Compliance: POSH reporting, policy document verification, and audit trail maintenance
  • Maternity Support: Return-to-work planning, AI-generated tips, and phase-based transition guidance
